Rules of Play

Game will consist of 3 rounds of play.

Each round, the players will roll a pair of dice to establish a round score.

Player with highest score after 3 rounds is declared the winner.

How to Score

If a player rolls a 1 on either die, round score is 0.

If a player rolls matching die values, round score is double the total of both dice.

Any other combinations, round score is the total of both dice.
